Hardware

The X6500 is designed to be a flexible, extendable device. It is sold bare, without an enclosure or accessories. We believe that designing the best mining device that suits everyone is impossible, so we want you to decide exactly how to set it up. Some people will want to enclose it in a nice looking box and set it in the living room as a conversation piece, with low noise being very important. Others will want to build it into a large cluster, with a carefully designed enclosure to maximize airflow to push the FPGAs to the limit. Others still will hack together something on their desk, or tuck it inside their PC tower. We love seeing the things people come up with! To make experimentation easy on you, we want to make available any and all information that could be useful to you.

Check back here for more information occasionally, or contact us if there is something you would like added.
