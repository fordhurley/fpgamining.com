The X6500 FPGA Miner is an FPGA coprocessor, utilizing dual Spartan-6 FPGAs for minimum cost and maximum power efficiency. It is optimized for Bitcoin mining, but can be adapted for other applications.

Typical perfomance achieved is 400 MH/s. Power consumption has been measured as low as 15 W. Because this is such a small amount of power, it can be easily kept cool with a single, low RPM fan for a nearly silent mining rig.
